Struct for containing vehicle thermal (and related) parameters.

Fields§

§fc_c_kj__k: f64

parameter fuel converter thermal mass [kJ/K]

§fc_l: f64

parameter for engine characteristic length [m] for heat transfer calcs

§fc_htc_to_amb_stop: f64

parameter for heat transfer coeff [W / (m ** 2 * K)] from eng to ambient during vehicle stop

§fc_coeff_from_comb: f64

coeff. for fraction of combustion heat that goes to fuel converter (engine) thermal mass. Remainder goes to environment (e.g. via tailpipe)

§tstat_te_sto_deg_c: f64

parameter for temperature [°C] at which thermostat starts to open

§tstat_te_delta_deg_c: f64

temperature delta [°C] over which thermostat is partially open

§rad_eps: f64

radiator effectiveness – ratio of active heat rejection from radiator to passive heat rejection

§fc_model: FcModelTypes

temperature-dependent efficiency fuel converter (engine or fuel cell) thermal model type

§ess_c_kj_k: f64

battery thermal mass [kJ/K]

§ess_htc_to_amb: f64

effective (incl. any thermal management system) heat transfer coefficient from battery to ambient

§cabin_hvac_model: CabinHvacModelTypes

cabin model internal or external w.r.t. fastsim

§cab_c_kj__k: f64

parameter for cabin thermal mass [kJ/K]

§cab_l_length: f64

cabin length [m], modeled as a flat plate

§cab_l_width: f64

cabin width [m], modeled as a flat plate

§cab_r_to_amb: f64

cabin shell thermal resistance [m **2 * K / W]

§cab_htc_to_amb_stop: f64

parameter for heat transfer coeff [W / (m ** 2 * K)] from cabin to ambient during vehicle stop

§exhport_model: ComponentModelTypes

‘external’ (effectively no model) is default exhaust port model type

§exhport_ha_to_amb: f64

thermal conductance [W/K] for heat transfer to ambient

§exhport_ha_int: f64

thermal conductance [W/K] for heat transfer from exhaust

§exhport_c_kj__k: f64

exhaust port thermal capacitance [kJ/K]

§cat_model: ComponentModelTypes§cat_l: f64

diameter [m] of catalyst as sphere for thermal model

§cat_c_kj__K: f64

catalyst thermal capacitance [kJ/K]

§cat_htc_to_amb_stop: f64

parameter for heat transfer coeff [W / (m ** 2 * K)] from catalyst to ambient during vehicle stop

§cat_te_lightoff_deg_c: f64

lightoff temperature to be used when fc_temp_eff_component == ‘hybrid’

§cat_fc_eta_coeff: f64

cat engine efficiency coeff. to be used when fc_temp_eff_component == ‘hybrid’

§orphaned: bool

for pyo3 api
